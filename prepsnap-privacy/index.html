Introduction

PrepSnap ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome extension that helps users prepare for job interviews by analysing job descriptions and generating AI-powered interview questions, company culture snapshots, and preparation checklists.

We are committed to transparency, minimal data usage, and protecting your privacy. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, what we do not collect, and how the extension operates.

By using PrepSnap, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection and Usage

2.1 Job Description Content

PrepSnap only processes job description data when you explicitly click "Generate Interview Pack".

  • Job description text is read from what you paste, highlight, or link — never collected passively or in the background
  • Sent to our backend (Supabase Edge Function) only on your explicit action
  • Forwarded to the Groq API solely to generate your interview pack
  • Never stored in any database or log after the response is returned
  • Discarded immediately after the AI response is delivered to your browser

2.2 Google Docs / Drive Links

If you provide a Google Docs or Drive link, our backend fetches the plain text content of that document server-side. This only works with documents set to "Anyone with the link can view." The link and document content are processed in memory and never stored.

2.3 Local Usage Data

The following data is stored only on your device using Chrome's local storage:

  • Your dark/light theme preference
  • Number of interview packs generated
  • Subscription status (free / Pro)
  • User consent confirmation
This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

2.4 Payment and License Verification

Payments are handled securely by Dodo Payments. We do not collect or store your payment card details. See Dodo Payments Privacy Policy.

When you use the Pro verification feature, you enter your purchase email in the side panel to unlock unlimited downloads and premium features. This email is sent to our verification system (Supabase) solely to confirm your Pro licence. It is not used for marketing, tracking, or any other purpose, and is not stored beyond the verification check.

2.5 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No browsing history
  • No passive or background data collection
  • No data from websites you visit other than what you explicitly provide
  • No audio or video recording of any kind
  • No personally identifiable information beyond what you voluntarily enter
  • No advertising tracking or behavioural profiling

Permissions Justification

PrepSnap requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

PermissionWhy it is needed
`activeTab` Allows the extension to open the side panel on the current tab when you click the toolbar icon
`contextMenus` Adds the "Analyze with PrepSnap" right-click option when you highlight text on any job page
`storage` Saves your theme preference, usage count, and subscription status locally on your device only
`sidePanel` Displays the interview pack and interface within Chrome's native side panel
No permissions are used for tracking, background surveillance, or accessing data beyond what you explicitly provide.

Third-Party Services

4.1 Groq API (AI Processing)

  • Used only when you click "Generate Interview Pack"
  • Processes your job description to generate interview questions, culture insights, and prep checklist
  • Does not retain or store user data after processing
  • Does not use your data for AI model training

See Groq Privacy Policy.

4.2 Supabase (Backend Infrastructure)

Our backend edge function is hosted on Supabase. Job description text passes through Supabase's infrastructure as part of the API call. Supabase does not retain this data on our behalf beyond the duration of the request.

See Supabase Privacy Policy.

4.3 Dodo Payments (Payment Processing)

All billing and payment processing is handled securely by Dodo Payments. We do not have access to your card details or full payment information.

See Dodo Payments Privacy Policy.

4.4 Google Fonts & Cloudflare CDN

The extension loads fonts from Google Fonts and the jsPDF library from Cloudflare's CDN for display and PDF generation purposes. No personal data is transmitted as part of these requests.

User Control and Consent

  • Data processing only begins after your explicit action — clicking "Generate Interview Pack"
  • You can clear all locally stored data by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome settings
  • You may stop using the extension at any time with no data consequences

Data Security

  • All communication between the extension and our backend uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • The Groq API key is stored as a server-side environment secret in Supabase and is never exposed in the extension's code or transmitted to your browser
  • No centralised storage of user content
  • Local-only data storage architecture for device preferences

Data Retention

  • Job description content is discarded immediately after your interview pack is generated
  • No long-term storage of any content you provide
  • Local device data (theme, usage count, subscription status) persists until you uninstall the extension or clear it manually via Chrome settings

User Rights

Since we do not store your personal data or job description content:

  • Uninstalling PrepSnap removes all locally stored data from your device
  • You can clear local data via Chrome's extension settings at any time
  • There is no account to delete as we do not create user accounts in v1.0

Children's Privacy

PrepSnap is designed for professional use — specifically for job seekers and professionals preparing for interviews. It is not targeted at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 13.

If you believe a minor has used this extension in a way that raises concerns, please contact us at arcovate.tech@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.
